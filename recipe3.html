    VEGETABLE SOUP

    Ingredients and Procedure

    INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 3/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup low fat vanilla yogurt
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Chia seeds

    • PROCEDURE

  • For smoothie:
      • Combine the berries, orange juice, yogurt, banana, and honey. In a blender and puree until smooth.
  • For the Toppings :
      • Pour the yumme smoothie in the bowl. Top with the Blueberries, raspberries with the chia seeds !

    Delicious VEGETABLE SOUP is ready!
